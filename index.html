After years of roasting and shipping coffee to the best customers we could have asked for, Pinky Promise Coffee Roasters has been sold and our online operations are now closed.

We are so grateful for your support. You chose to spend your coffee money with a small, community-focused roaster — and that meant the world to us.

The new owners are The Street Brew, longtime wholesale customers of ours who already love and believe in what we've been roasting. They're taking over both the cafe and roasting operations. Their plans for online sales are still taking shape, but we encourage you to follow along and show them some support:

If you had an active subscription, it has been cancelled — no further charges will be made to your card, and no additional shipments will be sent beyond any orders already in process.

Thank you, truly, for being part of the Pinky Promise story. — Sean Grorich, Pinky Promise Coffee Roasters
